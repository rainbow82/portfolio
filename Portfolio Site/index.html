Shannon Beach

Biography

I started out in graphic design, with a dream of working in 3D animation. Along the path, I discovered coding, fell in love, and haven't looked back since. The ability to bring ideas to life, to make them fully interactive, with a great user experience is thrilling.

I love web development, but have an undeniable passion for Artificial Intelligence (especially working with robotics or animatronics), and the Internet of Things. I believe in making the boring, everyday life tasks easier to manage with technology, and enjoy showing other people how to do the same.

When I'm not developing, I enjoy being outdoors, watching movies, and spending time with my family.

Projects

Beached Animal Rescue
Beached Animal Rescue is an Android app that guides users through what to do if they find a stranded sea mammal. The user's location is displayed using a location API, allowing them to give rescue workers an exact location of a stranded whale, dolphin, etc. Another screen displays information on what the user should and should not do to help the animal while waiting on rescue workers.
